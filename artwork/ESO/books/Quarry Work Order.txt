Quarry Work Order

By the will of Kurog, King in Orsinium, Over-Chief and Warlord of All Wrothgar,

— Fifty loads of premium stone from Clan Tumnosh's Graystone Quarry, to be delivered by the end of First Hunt.

— One-hundred more loads of equal-quality stone to be delivered before the year's end.

Advance payment, in the form of a Bill of Promise, delivered with this work order. Upon full delivery of stone, the Bill of Promise can be exchanged for an amount of gold equal to one-hundred-and-twenty-five percent of the stone's market value.

Failure to comply with or to complete this order will carry significant repercussions for Clan Tumnosh.
